Data Visualisation

For a week I have tracked how long I spend doing uni work, being on my phone or exercising every day.
I have organised the data in a table below and used canvas to represent it using three different types of graphs: bar chart, pie chart and a line graph
You can switch between the graphs by using the buttons underneath the canvas. You can also choose the colour scheme of the data using buttons on the right side.
